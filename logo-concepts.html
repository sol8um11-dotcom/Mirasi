Mirasi - Logo Concepts

Minimal & Modern | Meera (rebel poet) + Rasa (aesthetic emotion) | "Every face tells a legend"

Concept 1: The Rasa Drop

A drop shape (pigment/ink drop) with an eye at center - representing "rasa" (the emotional gaze). The drop is the essence of art flowing from tradition to screen.

App icon 32px Favicon

Concept 2: The Brush Stroke

The "M" is drawn as a single continuous brush stroke - like a miniature painter's hand. The gold dot at the end represents the final touch of gold leaf in Tanjore art.

App icon 32px

Concept 3: The Portrait Frame

A minimalist portrait frame with an abstract eye (rasa = the gaze that sees beauty) and a golden smile curve. Frame = art, Eye = seeing, Gold = Indian heritage.

App icon 32px

Concept 4: The Paisley Spiral

The paisley (or "buta") is the most iconic Indian art motif - found in every tradition from Kashmir to Kanchipuram. Here it's rendered as a minimal spiral, with a gold dot at the heart. Tradition meeting modernity.

App icon 32px

Concept 5: The Lotus Mark

Two petals forming a minimal lotus - the symbol of beauty emerging from the ordinary (just like your photo transforming into art). A solid dot at center = the "bindu" point of creation.

App icon 32px

Concept 6: The Palace Arch M

A geometric "M" shaped like the pointed arches of Indian palaces (Rajasthani jharokhas). The gold dot at the peak = a crown jewel. All-caps wordmark for authority. Ultra-clean.
